About Me

Hello World!

I am an aspiring full-stack developer with interests in web development, server side maintenance and VR/AR Development. I also enjoy many other types of programming fields such as mobile app development. Not only do I love to program, I love to not only listen to music but to play musical instructments as well. A food and travel enthusiast, can't seem to stay put. Unlike normal programming projects, web design allows me to see the results much faster. This was what drove me to pursue a career in the web development field. I have no artistic skills but do believe in clean, simple designs.
